Bad Data Science

Ben Ewing

2020/03/24

There's a lot of bad data science in the world.

  • FightCOVID19: To me this looks like a blatant attempt to profit (at least reputationally) from the COVID-19 pandemic. At the time I am writing this, it looks like the author of this repo has felt the need to build a massive ecosystem (Android/iOS/Web apps? Whos is this for?) around a pre-trained model updated with COVID-19 victim's chest scans. While using/updating pre-trained models is standard (and very often a good idea!), this project seems much more concerned with appearances than building a good model. This was brought to my attention by this (sigh) Reddit post.
